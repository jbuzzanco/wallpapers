OVERLYVISUAL WALLPAPER App

How to use: Find a Wallpaper you vibe with, then select set it to your Mac's desktop background by clicking the button below it.

Why it was made: To help you visualize BIG goals you might have in your life and to set them before your eyes on something you can look at everyday, whether it's your work MacBook or home computer. Visualization, even for a few minutes a day, and feeling the success, can help you on the road to actualizing success in your life in taking the actions to get to what you envision.

Made by: @joelondesign, an independent designer and developer, find me on Instagram and X.
